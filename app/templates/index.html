Linear Algebra Comics

I drew these comics in Fall of 2019 and Winter of 2020, during an (actually really good) linear algebra class. They sat in my notebooks for almost a year, until I decided others might find them funny, and so I made this website. Coding this website was my first real foray into HTML and CSS, and it has been a fun journey.

In the fall, I mostly drew short comics, relating to the day's lecture, or other things I thought were funny. I drew the (incredibly niche) Linear Algebra Parasite after seeing the film.
